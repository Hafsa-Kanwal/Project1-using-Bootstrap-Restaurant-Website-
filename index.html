Frequentely Ask Questions

~At what times can we have lunch and dinner in the restaurant?

You can ask for a table with a view and your request will be noted. However, we cannot guarantee it as the front-of-house manager decides the layout of the tables each day, based on the number of reservations.

~ Can we request a table with a view?

You can request a table with a view, and our team will do their best to accommodate. However, please note that table assignments depend on the front-of-house manager's daily layout based on reservations.

~ Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, our menu includes a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Please ask your server for recommendations or consult the menu for detailed options.

~ Are reservations required?

Reservations are recommended, especially during peak hours, but walk-ins are also welcome based on availabilityor consult the menu for detailed options.
